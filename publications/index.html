Event-driven Tactile Sensing With Dense Spiking Graph Neural Networks

Fangming Guo, Fangwen Yu, Mingyan li, et al.

IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement, 2025

This work proposes DeepTactile, a spiking graph neural network designed for event-driven tactile sensing. By structuring tactile data as graphs based on the local connectivity of taxels and leveraging spiking graph convolutional networks for feature extraction, the method effectively handles event-based learning. Experimental results on three event-based tactile datasets demonstrate its superiority over state-of-the-art techniques.
